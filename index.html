PEOPLE’S REPUBLIC OF BANGLADESH FLAG RULES 1. Title and date of effect: (a) These Rules shall be known as the People’s Republic of Bangladesh Flag Rules. 1972. (b) They shall come into effect immediately. 2. Definitions: In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires1. ‘Flag’ means the ‘National Flag’ of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh. 3. Description and size of the ‘Flag’: The ‘National Flag’ will be in bottle green and rectangular in size in the proportion of length to width 10: 6 bearing a red circle on the body of the green. The red circle will have a radius of one-fifth of the length of the flag. Its center will be placed on the intersecting point of the perpendicular drawn from the nine-twentieth part of the length of the flag and the horizontal line drawn through the middle of its width. I. Colour- (a) The green base of the flag will be of Procion Brilliant Green H-2RS 50 parts per 1000. (b) The red circular part will be of Procion Brilliant Orange H-2RS 60 parts per 1000. II. Size of the ‘Flag’ for Building- (a) 10'×6' (b) 5'×3' (c)2½'×1½' [Depending on the size of the Building and in case of necessity, a flag of bigger size maintaining the proportion of length and width may be allowed by the Government to be flown.] III.
